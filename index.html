Research
Master's Thesis

Structure Prediction for Chemically Modified Peptides

Developing an end-to-end pipeline combining learned conformer generation, sequence-based structure prediction, and physics-based refinement to enable 3D modelling of peptides with non-canonical residues. Benchmarked against AlphaFold 3 and PEPstrMOD across curated datasets.

Prof. G.P.S. Raghava, IIIT Delhi AlphaFold 2 · ET-Flow · MACE-OFF23 2025 – 2026
Academic Project

Skin Disease Classification Using Deep Learning

Designed and implemented a deep learning pipeline for image classification and video anomaly detection using CNNs. Applied OpenCV and TensorFlow to build real-time object detection and tracking models.

IISER Pune CNN · OpenCV · TensorFlow Jan – Apr 2025
Semester Project

Mechanism Underlying Root Regeneration (DNRR)

Studied how autophagy affects root regeneration by generating double mutants of ATG8F × ATG8H. Constructed YUCCA4 and ATHB8 tagged with NLS-NeonGreen to investigate auxin synthesis and procambium stem cell patterning.

PI: Prof. Kalika Prasad, IISER Pune Plant Genetics · Confocal Microscopy Jun – Nov 2024
Semester Project

Mitochondrial Genome Analysis: Function and Evolution

Analysed mitochondrial genomes across species, wrote a pipeline to identify potential protein sequences, and used BLAST to discover novel proteins in yeast mitochondrial genomes.

PI: Prof. M.S. Madhusudhan, IISER Pune BLAST · ClustalW · Python Aug – Dec 2023
